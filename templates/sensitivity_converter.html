About Sensitivity Conversion

Mouse sensitivity conversion allows you to maintain the same muscle memory when switching between different games. Each game engine handles sensitivity differently, making accurate conversion essential for competitive play.

Our converter uses proven mathematical formulas and community-tested conversion ratios to ensure the most accurate results possible.

Understanding Sensitivity Metrics

Different games use different measurement systems for sensitivity:

  • Counter-Strike 2/Valorant: Simple sensitivity multiplier (typically 0.5-3.0)
  • Fortnite: Separate X and Y sensitivity settings with different scaling
