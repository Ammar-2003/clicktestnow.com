Understanding eDPI

Your effective DPI (eDPI) is the true sensitivity measurement that matters for muscle memory development. It's calculated by multiplying your mouse DPI by your in-game sensitivity setting.

Why eDPI is important:

  • Consistency Across Games: Maintain the same feel regardless of the game you're playing
  • Precision Aiming: Find the perfect balance between speed and accuracy
  • Professional Comparison: Compare your settings with pro players to benchmark your setup

Professional eDPI Ranges

While personal preference plays a big role, most professional gamers use these eDPI ranges:

  • CS:GO/Valorant: 200-1200 eDPI (lower for precision)
  • Overwatch: 3000-8000 eDPI (higher for tracking)
  • Fortnite: 100-200 eDPI (varies based on playstyle)
  • Apex Legends: 800-1600 eDPI (balance of precision and movement)

Remember that these are just guidelines. The best eDPI is what feels most comfortable and effective for you.
